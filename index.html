Section 2

Why We're Doing This

😤 Pain Point
❓"I don't know who to ask for governance help"
👤"Office hours never have the right person"
📭"I missed the session and can't find the answer"
🕳️"There's no record of decisions made in office hours"
✅ Our Solution
🤖EDG Slackbot provides instant answers + expert routing via Slack workflow
🔄Coverage rotation with expertise tagging ensures the right SME is always available
📋Every session auto-summarized and posted; living Canvas is the SSOT
🗄️Async audit trail + AI-fed knowledge base captures every decision

💡 Inspired by: How teams like Tableau Next, Enterprise Architecture, and Data Domain run their programs — plus a "self-serve before you book" model powered by Slack AI.

Section 3

How We Got Here: Options Evaluated

OPTION A Evaluated
📚

Strategic Learning Sessions

Lunch-and-learn format with curated agendas and presentations. Deep dives into a single governance topic per session.

✦ Best for: education and awareness

OPTION B Evaluated
🚪

Open Access Drop-In

No agenda, all SMEs from each pillar available in an open session. Flexible but potentially unstructured and unpredictable.

✦ Best for: ad hoc support

OPTION C Evaluated
🎯

Expert-Matched Consultations

Slack workflow routes each question to the most qualified EDG member by expertise area. High-quality answers, lower throughput.

✦ Best for: complex questions

OUR DECISION ✅ Selected
🧩

Hybrid Model

AI self-serve as the first layer. Async workflow for structured intake. On-demand expert sessions triggered only by escalation. Best of all three — lowest friction, highest coverage, no standing meetings.

✦ Best for: everything
